Shelves 1

The first bookshelves I have purpose made for the curved walls at Tyle House Oast. These needed to be simple in form to cope with our robust oast house interior. They also needed to be part adjustable and strong enough to support (some of) our ever growing collection of books. The shelves and uprights are made from wide (300mm) planks of Sweet Chestnut with interlocking joints and a minimum of fixings, finished in water based varnish and furniture polish to keep the tone of the wood light and the beautiful grain strong.



Components, 2 uprights and 7 shelves.



Assembly with a minimum of fixings.



And in place on the curved wall of the roundel room.
